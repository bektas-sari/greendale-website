About Greendale

A rich history, a bright future

Our Story

Founded in 1892 as a small farming community, Greendale has grown into a thriving city of 45,000 residents while maintaining its small-town charm and strong community values. Our city was originally established around the convergence of three pristine rivers, which provided fertile soil for agriculture and clean water for early settlers.

Today, Greendale stands as a model of sustainable urban development, balancing economic growth with environmental stewardship. Our downtown district features beautifully preserved historic buildings alongside modern amenities, creating a unique blend of old and new that residents and visitors cherish.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional municipal services while fostering a safe, inclusive, and prosperous community for all residents.

Our Vision

To be a premier destination for families and businesses, recognized for our innovation, sustainability, and quality of life.

Municipal Services

Comprehensive services for our community

Waste Management

Comprehensive recycling and waste disposal services, including weekly curbside pickup, hazardous waste collection, and community composting programs.

Parks & Recreation

Over 15 parks, community centers, sports facilities, and year-round recreational programs for all ages, promoting active and healthy lifestyles.

Public Safety

24/7 police and fire protection, emergency medical services, and community safety programs ensuring the security and well-being of all residents.

Public Works

Infrastructure maintenance, road construction, snow removal, and utility services maintaining our city's essential systems and transportation networks.

Education Support

Partnership with local schools, library services, after-school programs, and educational initiatives supporting lifelong learning in our community.

Health & Wellness

Public health programs, senior services, youth development initiatives, and mental health resources promoting community well-being and support.
